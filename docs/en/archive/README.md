# Archive

Historical design notes and implementation plans for `boid`.

These documents are **point-in-time snapshots** and may not reflect the current implementation. For the up-to-date model, see the [Guide](../guide/) and (when published) the [Reference](../reference/).

The original notes are written in Japanese and live under [`../ja/archive/`](../ja/archive/). They are kept for context on past design decisions; new contributors who do not read Japanese should rely on the English [Concepts](../guide/concepts.md) and [State machine](../guide/state-machine.md) pages instead.
